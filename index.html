Before / After

The goal is not to add more tools. The goal is to remove friction.

Before automation

  • Messages are handled manually
  • Leads get lost between chats and sheets
  • The team repeats the same answers
  • No clear handoff or follow-up logic

After automation

  • Customers get structured replies faster
  • Qualified leads are saved automatically
  • The team sees clean context
  • Follow-up becomes part of the system
